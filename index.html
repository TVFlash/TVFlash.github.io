Features

Bringing body and sole together.

Predictive Coaching

Lift smarter, bike longer and run like never before with a personal trainer in your shoes. Our patent pending training algorithms don't just display your workout data, they help you improve.

Discrete Design

Keep your mind on your workout, not on a screen. Solemate gives you directions, alerts and coaching all from the bottom of your feet.

Workout Rewards™

Every time you workout with Solemate gets you closer to great savings at our partners such as Foot Locker, Finish Line, Greater Boston Running store and more!

Mission Statement

We are committed to creating only the best health and fitness tracker on the market. Solemate Compromise is not a word in our vocabulary.

About

Our timeline.

  • August 2015

    Our Humble Beginnings

    The Solemate team first met at Purdue University in Introduction to Entrepreneurship and Innovation. After spending a few weeks brainstorming, they decided to pursue the creation of a wearable fitness tracker.

  • January 2016

    A Company is Born

    The first of the year marked the foundation of a new venture, Solemate LLC.

  • April 2016

    Launch of Solemate

    The first products rolled of the line

  • August 2016

    Corporate Expansion

    Solemate LLC's Global Headquarters is established in San Francisco
